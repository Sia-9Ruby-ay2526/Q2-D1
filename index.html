Emergency Go Bag

Maria Agatha Sia | 9-Ruby

Medication and First Aid

You should have medicines in your go bag because in cases where you contract an illness, you may not be able to access a pharmacy during an emergency. Hence, you will need to address ailments, injuries, and chronic conditions on your own.

Personal Items

Having personal items in your go bag is important for meeting your own individual needs and preserving your identification records.

Food and Water

You should always have food and water to prevent dehydration and malnutrition.

Bag

You should have a bag to store all your tools and supplies.
